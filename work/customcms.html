Friend Finder

This is a Friend Finding App where users are given questions and then provided with a match in the form of a gif of a celebrity or character in TV or music. The logic behind this game is that it takes the user’s info and compares it to the characters answers. Whomever the user’s answers match to, they will receive a gif of that character.
