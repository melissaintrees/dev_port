With my veggie burger tracker, users can order from the menu, save their own creations, and “eat” their orders. It was built with the idea of raising awareness to the fact that veggie burgers are a healthy and environmentally friendly alternative to meat burgers. Inspired by The Mohawk Bend’s site, the app is designed to be clean, minimal, and responsive.

Built with: Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node, Express, AJAX, MySQL, Handlebars
