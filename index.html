I’m Melissa St Moore,
a Front End Developer and Designer.

As a creative thinker, I research, build and iterate upon software experiences leveraging modern technologies and data. Getting started coding websites to promote my creative ventures, I went on to build sites and apps for brands such as Michelin, T-Mobile, Western Union and Under Armour as well as developed a site for Next Now Agency that gets over 3,000 views per month. I'm currently freelancing at Acclivus, a non-profit that helps reduce violence in underserved communities in Chicago.

I’m passionate about building device-friendly apps and sites, empathetic, always learning, and driven by a goal to help people with software. As a musical artist, I bring a fresh approach to problem solving and a fun way of collaborating with teams.
